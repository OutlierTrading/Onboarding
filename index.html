Membership policies

Important operating expectations.

These principles protect the quality of the community, member discussions, proprietary materials, and the educational purpose of the offering.

Educational purpose only Videos, livestreams, written materials, tools, templates, discussions, workshops, and coaching are educational and informational. They are not personalized financial, legal, investment, or tax advice.
No investment advice or fiduciary relationship Outlier Trading is not a registered investment adviser, broker-dealer, or financial planner. Members remain responsible for their own trading and investing decisions.
No guarantees Trading and investing involve risk. No result is promised or guaranteed, and past performance does not predict future outcomes.
Intellectual property and confidentiality Member materials, livestreams, workshops, discussions, direct communications, and coaching content may not be recorded, redistributed, resold, publicly summarized, or replicated without written permission.
Benefit changes Membership schedules, tiers, features, and benefits may be modified, paused, or discontinued. Reasonable notice will be provided when practical.
Coaching scheduling Coaching sessions do not carry forward between months. Rescheduling should occur more than 24 hours before the call; late changes may result in the monthly session being forfeited.
